Travel Guide to San Francisco

Take a ride in a trolley!

Drive down one of the top tourist attractions Lombard Street! Lombard street was built to increase the safety of the street because the street was very steep.

Walk on the Golden Gate Bridge from San Francisco to a small city named Sausalito

Take a boat to the notorious prison Alcatraz! Alcatraz is known for where the most dangerous prisoners were kept in. The prison had to shut down because running the facility was too expensive. 36 prisoners tried escaping and only 3 escaped successfully.
